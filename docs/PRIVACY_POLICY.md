# Privacy Policy for SleepGuardian

SleepGuardian built the SleepGuardian app as an open-source/academic project. This SERVICE is provided by the development team at Rzeszow University of Technology at no cost and is intended for use as is.

### Information Collection and Use
For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to User Email (for authentication purposes). The information that we request will be retained on our secure cloud servers hosted on Render.

### Hardware Permissions
The app requires local access to device hardware components to execute its core features:
- **Camera (Flashlight):** Used solely to trigger the stroboscopic penalty mode. No images or videos are captured or transmitted.
- **System Overlay (Draw over other apps):** Used to render the screen locker curtain during active sleep sessions.
- **Device Sensors (Accelerometer):** Used locally to monitor phone orientation and physical movement.

### Security
We value your trust in providing us your personal data, thus we are using commercially acceptable means of protecting it (including JWT authentication and bcrypt password hashing).
